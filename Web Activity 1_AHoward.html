Nutmeg Spice

What is Nutmeg Spice?

Nutmeg is a spice made from the seed of the nutmeg tree (Myristica fragrant), a native Indonesian evergreen tree that is the source of two popular spices: nutmeg and mace.
Nutmeg is the inner seed, while mace is the red, lace-like substance that covers the seed.

Nutmeg Spice French Toast

Ingredients for Nutmeg Spice French Toast

1 loaf of white bread
5 eggs plus 3 egg yolks
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 cup half-and-half or milk
1/2 cup plain yogurt, preferably Greek
3 tablespoons granulated sugar
2 teaspoons ground nutmeg, plus 1/8 teaspoon
Butter or canola oil for frying
2 tablespoons powdered sugar

If you want fluffy French toast you may add greater or less amount of eggs.
